How It All Began

Founded in 2014 by environmental scientist Dr. Sarah Chen, Squiggles started as a small research project in her garage. Frustrated by the lack of truly eco-friendly cleaning options that actually worked, Sarah set out to create something different.

After two years of research and development, our first product - the All-Purpose Cleaner - was born. What started as a solution for her own home quickly became a favorite among friends and family, and the demand grew organically.

Today, Squiggles has grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of households across the country, but we've never lost sight of our original mission: creating cleaning products that work brilliantly while protecting our planet.
